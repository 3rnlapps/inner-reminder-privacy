Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Inner Reminder (“the App”) handles information when you use it.

Inner Reminder is designed as a privacy-first reminders app. The App helps you create collections of prompts and receive gentle reminders on your device.

Developer contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request deletion of support data you sent to us, contact:

3rnl.apps@gmail.com

Data stored on your device

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Collections you create
  • Prompts you add
  • Schedule settings
  • Reminder history needed for notification scheduling
  • Prompt reaction and priority information, such as Like, Neutral, or Dislike
  • App settings, including onboarding completion and Premium entitlement status

This data is stored locally on your device. We do not run a server that automatically receives your prompts, collections, schedules, or reactions.

Prompts and collections

Your prompt text and collection names are private user content.

The App does not intentionally upload prompt text or collection names to our servers.

If you choose to send a support report, the report is designed not to include prompt text or collection names.

Notifications

The App uses Android notification permissions to send scheduled reminders.

Notifications are generated on your device based on your enabled collections, enabled prompts, and schedule settings.

The App may store local technical records about planned, delivered, skipped, or dismissed reminders so notification scheduling can work reliably.

Support reports

The App includes a “Report a problem” feature. This feature is optional and user-initiated.

When you choose to send a support report, the App creates a local archive that may include technical diagnostic information such as:

  • App version
  • Android version
  • Device model
  • Timezone
  • Notification permission state
  • Battery optimization state
  • Notification channel state
  • Number of collections
  • Number of enabled schedules
  • Reminder occurrence IDs, times, statuses, and skip or failure reasons
  • A local support correlation ID

Support reports are created locally and are shared only when you choose to send them, for example by email.

Support reports are intended to help diagnose notification scheduling issues. They should not include prompt text or collection names.

If you send us a support report by email, your email address and the report attachment will be processed for support purposes.

Purchases and Premium

The App offers a one-time Premium purchase through Google Play Billing.

Purchase processing is handled by Google Play. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

The App may store a local Premium entitlement state on your device so Premium features remain unlocked.

Google Play may process purchase-related information according to Google’s own policies.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your prompt text, collections, schedules, or reactions with advertisers.

Data may be shared only in the following cases:

  • With Google Play, when you use Google Play Billing
  • With your email provider or chosen sharing app, if you manually send a support report
  • If required by law or valid legal process

Permissions used by the App

The App may request or use the following Android permissions:

  • Notifications: to show reminder notifications
  • Receive boot completed: to restore scheduled reminders after device restart
  • Battery optimization settings: to help you configure Android so reminders can arrive more reliably
  • Internet and network state: used by Google Play Billing and related Google Play services

Data retention

Data stored locally in the App remains on your device until you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the App.

Support reports sent by email may be retained as long as reasonably needed to investigate and respond to support requests. You can request deletion of support emails and attachments by contacting 3rnl.apps@gmail.com.

Data deletion

You can delete your local data by:

  • Deleting individual prompts or collections in the App
  • Clearing app data in Android settings
  • Uninstalling the App

To request deletion of support data you previously emailed to us, contact:

3rnl.apps@gmail.com

Security

The App is designed to keep your core content on your device.

Support reports are shared only when you initiate sharing. Please avoid writing sensitive personal information in support emails unless necessary.

Children

Inner Reminder is not directed to children.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and publish the updated policy.
